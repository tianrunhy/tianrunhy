Monetization Models

Two complementary revenue engines

Every app in our portfolio operates on a hybrid IAA & IAP stack, mediated through layered ad platforms and a strict UX-first ads policy.

A · IAA — In-App Advertising

User-respectful advertising

Light-touch open-screen, rewarded video, interstitial and banner placements — scheduled around the user's natural attention budget. Frequency caps, child-directed safety and contextual filters by default.

  • Google AdMob, Meta Audience Network, AppLovin MAX
  • Unity Ads, ironSource, Vungle, Chartboost, AdColony
  • InMobi, Tapjoy, StartApp, Pinterest, TikTok, Snap, Reddit
  • Google "Families" compliant ads configuration
B · IAP — In-App Purchase

Premium unlocks & subscriptions

One-time unlocks, consumable credits and auto-renewing subscriptions, processed through Google Play Billing v6 and Apple StoreKit 2 with server-side receipt verification.

  • Google Play Billing Library v6
  • Apple StoreKit 2 server notifications
  • Paywalls, free trial & graceful downgrade UX
  • Local receipt validation & audit logs
C · Cross-platform Engineering

Native & shared code

Kotlin / Swift for performance-critical paths, Jetpack Compose & SwiftUI for UI, plus Flutter / React Native bridges. App size budgets kept under 30 MB.

  • Kotlin & Jetpack Compose (Android 8+)
  • Swift & SwiftUI (iOS 15+)
  • Flutter 3.x & React Native bridges
  • ML Kit, TensorFlow Lite, Core ML
D · Compliance & Policy

Privacy by design & by default

GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, LGPD, PIPEDA, UK GDPR, COPPA & Google Play Families — engineered and audited from day one, in 120+ jurisdictions.

  • Data Protection Impact Assessments (DPIA)
  • Age-gating, COPPA, GDPR-K verification
  • CMP & TCF v2.2 readiness
  • ISO 27001-style SDLC checkpoints
