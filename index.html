Frequently Asked

Common questions, honest answers.

Everything you need to know before we start a conversation. Need something more specific? Reach out.

Do you really not collect user data?

Correct — the user-generated content inside our apps (routes, journals, habit logs, receipts, collections) lives entirely on-device by default. We don't run a backend that mirrors it. Aggregated, anonymized diagnostics and billing receipts are the only exceptions, and both are documented in detail in our Privacy Policy.

How long does a typical mobile app engagement take?

Most of our portfolio apps shipped in 8–14 weeks from kick-off to first public release. That assumes a clear scope and weekly design reviews. White-label co-development and complex enterprise projects range from 3 to 9 months. We'll always give you an honest range after the discovery call.

Do you publish on both Google Play and the Apple App Store?

Yes. We are an established Google Play developer with a Tier-1 ad mediation stack and full Designed for Families certification. Our iOS builds use Apple StoreKit 2 with App Privacy Labels and the App Tracking Transparency prompt. We also publish on Huawei AppGallery, Amazon Appstore, Samsung Galaxy Store and Microsoft Store where it makes commercial sense.

What does your monetization stack look like?

A hybrid IAA + IAP model mediated by AppLovin MAX, with primary networks including Google AdMob, Meta Audience Network, Unity Ads, ironSource, Pangle, AppLovin Exchange and InMobi. All ad placements are frequency-capped, contextual by default, and disabled for users under 13 in compliance with Google Play Families and COPPA. IAP is processed via Google Play Billing v6 or Apple StoreKit 2 with server-side receipt verification.

Can you sign an NDA, MSA or DPA before we share specs?

Absolutely. We have a master MSA template that can be turned around within 48 hours, and we execute bilateral NDAs and DPAs (incorporating EU SCCs, the UK Addendum, or China CAC Standard Contract) on request. Our DPO is reachable at .

Where is your team, and do you offer follow-the-sun support?

Headquartered in Beijing with engineering hubs in Manchester and Lisbon. Customer support runs 09:00–18:00 (UTC+8) on weekdays with a follow-the-sun incident-response rotation covering 24/7 for production-critical services.

How do you handle compliance with GDPR, CCPA, LGPD and PIPL?

Privacy is engineered, not bolted on. We run Data Protection Impact Assessments before every release, honor GPC and ATT signals, and use TCF v2.2-compliant CMPs. Regional residency options include EU-Frankfurt, US (multiple regions), APAC, and PRC-localized storage with CAC-approved cross-border transfer mechanisms.

Can I see a sample app before committing?

Yes — all six apps in our portfolio are live on Google Play. The Outdoor Offline Route Resolver and the Periodic Habit Tracker are great entry points to evaluate our craft, performance, and ad mediation feel. Direct APK previews for white-label partners are available under NDA.
